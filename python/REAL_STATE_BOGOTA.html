GOALS

About Data

Data from 2023.
9520 records in the initial data.
9 variables.
7 zones of the city.

About Variables

  • Description: The description of the property that is in sale
  • Value: Price of the property
  • id: Identificator
  • Neighborhood: District or community in the city
  • Localidad: Zone bigger than Neighborhood
  • Rooms: The number of Rooms that has the property
  • Bathrooms: The number of Barthrooms that has the property
  • Area: Space that has the property in square meters

Duration

This project took about six months including the develop of the present web page.

Mean of Values According to the Zone

We can see that if we base our concept in means, the most expesive properties in Bogota
are located in the East Zone of the city, followed by the North Zone
while the cheapest zone is the Southwest Zone

Mean of Areas According to the Zone

The most big properties in Bogota
are located in the South, East and North Zone of the city
while the smallest area by mean is the Southwest Zone.

Number of Properties According to the Number of Rooms

The majority of properties in Bogota have 3 or 2 rooms.

Conclusions


- The averages values of the East and North zones are the most expensive,
while the South and the Southwest have the lowest values.

- Except the Southwest Zone, the mean of Areas of the properties are too similar.
